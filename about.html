Students' Federation of India

SFI GEC

Students Federation of India, commonly known as SFI, is a student's organisation that stands for the establishment of a progressive, democratic and egalitarian education system. Founded in 1970, SFI is one of India's largest student organisation. It has built a powerful and well-knit student movement for the upliftment and betterment of the student community. With its slogan 'Independence, Democracy, Socialism', SFI stands for the advancement of a socialist India that ensures social and economic equality to all. It also seeks to organise the student community in the struggles of the wider democratic movement of the workers, peasants, and other progressive forces. SFI strives to unite students against authoritarianism and stifling of democracy. Moreover, SFI seeks to educate and enable students with the means to fight against exploitation, oppression and discrimination.

SFI GEC, is the Students Federation of India unit from Government Engineering College, Thrissur. From its establishment, SFI GEC has consistently undertaken burning issues of vital concern to the student community. The organisation's successful intervention in various problems has resulted in its immense popularity on the campus. SFI GEC fully incorporates the 'Study and Struggle' motto. From politics to science, from events to strikes, SFI GEC has always been for the students by the students. The organisation and its duties are not limited to the campus. SFI GEC has always taken a political stand in both national and international issues, even organizing strikes to agitate and educate. With its active social media presence, SFI GEC encourages young engineers to choose the progressive socialist way of life.
